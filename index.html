Hi, I'm Rattus

I believe we are born to dream, and to share that dream. This website is a place for me to share mine.

My main hobbies are in Computing and Music. In computing, I enjoy programming and reverse engineering, and when it comes to choice of language I prefer C/C++ due to how it functions at a low-level.

These loves stem from my experiences in rom-hacking, most prominently Pikmin 2. While I have yet to release a rom-hack of this game, it's still a dream I wish to show you one day.

As for music, I started learning to play the violin at a young age. Though I never really engaged deeply with it until around 2020, that was when I realized I could play scores from my favourite games, the first of these scores that caught my attention was "Hornet - Hollow Knight".

Music from games continue to be my main focus to this day, the way that they can tell stories, and the way you tie memories to these songs is pretty cool in my opinion. They have inspired me to compose my own music, akin to the many soundtracks I've heard over the years. Each track I create, I try and tell a story, of a place, person, or moment in history.

Aside from the violin, I have become fluent on both ocarina and my special 42 key kalimba. Sometime the future, I also wish to learn the piano.
